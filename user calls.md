 # User Research: Battery Dashboard Audit                                                                                                                                                        
  ## User Calls — 24th April                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                  
  ### Key Findings                                                                                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                  
  1. **Google Form** is used to log charged/discharged batteries                                                                                                                                  
  2. **WhatsApp group chat** is used to communicate idle/discharged battery clearance
  3. IAs call managers to report extra/faulty batteries                                                                                                                                           
  4. **Warehouse-tagged batteries are all visible in self audit**
  5. Battery has two states: **battery status** (location) and **battery state** (charging status)                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                  
  ### Deployed vs. Idle Bikes                                                                                                                                                                     
  - Deployed bike → no battery audit needed                                                                                                                                                       
  - Bike not in use → battery is removed and kept aside

  ### Open Questions — Answered                                                                                                                                                                   
   
  **1. How frequently are IAs supposed to do self audit?**                                                                                                                                        
  At the start and end of each shift, in front of the next IA who is resuming/ending their shift.
                                                                                                                                                                                                  
  **2. What type of batteries appear in self audit?**
  All types. The IA scans all warehouse batteries (100–200 per audit). Scanned batteries get their location updated; missing ones are flagged.                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                  
  **3. What screens exist in the self audit flow?**                                                                                                                                               
  - **Screen 1 — Location Validation:** Validates the IA's location against the warehouse location                                                                                                
  - **Screen 2 — Scanner:** IA scans batteries one by one                                                                                                                                         
  - **Screen 3 — Battery List:** Shows Remaining, Audited, and Extra batteries (e.g. YA002699). IA taps "End Battery Audit" when done                                                             
  - **Screen 4 — Missing Battery Modal:** If batteries are unscanned, shows "X battery is missing!" and notifies the manager (e.g. Chandan Prabhakar) to find it or report to the control room    
                                                                                                                                                                                                  
  **4. How many batteries are shown in the audit?**                                                                                                                                               
  Varies — typically 100–200 batteries per audit session.
