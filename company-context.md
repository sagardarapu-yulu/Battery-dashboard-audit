# Yulu

## Why We Exist
Yulu is reimagining urban mobility by building new infrastructure for last-mile movement of people and goods — using purpose-built electric vehicles, an owned energy network, and technology that ties it all together.

## How We're Organized (9 Product Pods)

### Demand Pods
1. **Mobility** — All people & goods mobility business lines
2. **Logistics** — YuMove Network (Earn with Yulu, Courier)

### Supply Pods
3. **Service Operations** — Access & availability (vehicle supply at Yulu Centers, swaps, rebalancing)
4. **Field & Workforce Operations** — Ground execution (shifts, task allocation, on-ground staff)
5. **Fleet Health & Maintenance** — Reliability & safety (repairs, QC, fault handling)
6. **Asset & Parts Lifecycle (Supply Chain)** — Procurement, part logistics, inventory, assembly, refurbishment

### Platform Pods
7. **Payments**
8. **Yulu Business Partner**

## Business Model

| Model | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Per Minute | Pay-per-use rides |
| Hourly | Hourly ride packages |
| Daily | Daily ride packages |
| Weekly Rentals | Weekly vehicle rental |
| Ownership (Vehicle + BaaS) | Users buy vehicle upfront or via EMI + monthly subscription for Battery as a Service |
| B2B Monthly Billing | Fleet-level monthly billing for enterprise |
| YBP (Yulu Business Partner) | Full asset + platform to manage assets — like Yulu but for different fleet owners |
